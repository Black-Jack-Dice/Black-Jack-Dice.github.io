Black Jack Dice

Blackjack Dice Game Rules

GAMEPLAY

The objective of the game is to be the first player to score 10 points. Each player gets two 6-sided dice to play. In each turn, the current player rolls two dice and scores as following:

  • In each turn, the current player rolls two dice and adds the dice numbers to his/her/their score IN THAT TURN. The player can roll as many times as he/she/they want/s.
  • Once the current player has scored 16 or more, he/she/they has/have the option to stop rolling, or just to roll one die one or more times.
  • If the turn total goes over 21, then the player is busted and gets no points for that turn.
  • The aim is to score exactly 21 or get closest to 21 without going over.
  • If the current player scores exactly 21 in the round, then he/she/they immediately get/s 2 points.
  • If no player scores 21, the player closest to 21 gets 1 point. If both players have the same score, no points are given.
  • The game is played for multiple rounds and the first player to reach 10 points wins the game.
